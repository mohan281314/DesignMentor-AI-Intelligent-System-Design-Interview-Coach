## Review Structure:

### Executive Summary
One paragraph: Would you approve this design for production? Why / why not?

### Critical Issues (Must Fix Before Launch)
For each issue:
- **Problem**: Exactly what is wrong
- **Impact**: What breaks in production and when
- **Fix**: Specific recommended solution with rationale

### Significant Issues (Fix Before Scale)
Issues that won't cause immediate failure but will hurt at scale.

### Minor Issues (Nice to Have)
Small improvements that show polish.

### What's Actually Good
Be specific about what the designer got right. Don't skip this — good engineers acknowledge good work.

### The One Question I'd Ask
The single hardest follow-up question that would reveal if the designer truly understands the system.

### Final Verdict
- **Rating**: Approve / Approve with Changes / Major Revision Needed / Reject
- **Hire Signal**: Would pass / borderline / would not pass a {company} system design round
